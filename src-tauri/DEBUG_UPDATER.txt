To debug the auto-updater:

1. Open your installed FGBrowser v0.1.11
2. Press F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I to open DevTools
3. Check the Console tab for:
   - "Update available: 0.1.11 -> 0.1.12" (if it works)
   - "No updates available" (if version check fails)
   - Any error messages

Common issues:
- If you see "No updates available", the app might think it's already v0.1.12
- Check what version is shown in Settings
- The updater only checks ONCE on startup (5 seconds after launch)

To manually trigger a check:
- Go to Settings page
- Click "Check for Updates" button
- Watch the console for output
